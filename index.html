Gabrielle Union-Wade

Your future first female black president of America. She'll make the country be the best it could be

Gabrielle Monique Union-Wade is an American actress, activist, and author. She began her career in the 1990s, appearing on television sitcoms, before landing supporting roles in teenage comedic films She's All That and 10 Things I Hate About You. Now she wants to help our country get out of this dark place post Trump.
